The intended outcomes of this project are: 

• A research publication sharing aggregated, anonymized findings from our interviews. The goal here is to shine a light on the motivations behind providing resources and tools that support the experiences of Black people, the impact these resources and tools have on the community, and where there may be opportunities for others to provide support to these kind of initiatives.
 
• Amplification of the amazing, impactful contributions being made for Black people by Black people. If there’s interest from participants, we intend to share the curated contributions (and contributors) from our study with our community.
