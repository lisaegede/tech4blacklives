Resources and Commonly Asked Questions

What is the focus of the research on Black people designing technology?

The research focuses on understanding how Black people design technology for their lived experiences.

What methodology does the research team use?

The research team uses a mixed methodology approach to study motivations, impact, and opportunities for support in building resources and tools.
